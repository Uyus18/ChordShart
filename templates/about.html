Our Story

GuitarChart started with one idea in mind: to make guitar shopping easier, safer, and more accessible to everyone in the Philippines. Whether you’re a beginner buying your first instrument or an experienced player searching for your next upgrade, we want to simplify your journey.

We saw how many musicians struggled with overpriced stores, low-quality products, and unreliable sellers. That's why GuitarChart was created — a trusted online shop where quality and affordability meet.

Today, GuitarChart continues to grow because of our passion for music and our commitment to providing customers with guitars they can truly rely on.

Our Mission

At GuitarChart, our mission is to bring music closer to every home by offering quality guitars at honest prices.

We aim to provide a hassle-free online shopping experience — from browsing, ordering, secure checkout, QR payment options, up to door-to-door delivery.

Most importantly, we want every customer to feel supported and confident in every purchase, knowing that GuitarChart values trust, service, and passion for music.
